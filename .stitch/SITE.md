# Site Vision: VSG Bliss

**Stitch Project ID:** 14081161961353062505

## 1. Project Overview

VSG Bliss is a multimedia entertainment brand website featuring music, events, and artist content with a "Modern Speakeasy" luxury aesthetic.

## 2. Site Vision

A dark, immersive digital lounge experience that captures the essence of late-night music culture. Premium feel with burgundy/gold accents and editorial typography.
