A music streaming section for VSG Bliss featuring artist tracks and playlists.

**DESIGN SYSTEM (REQUIRED):**
- Background: Charcoal Black (#131313)
- Primary Container: Burgundy Wine (#4E0707)
- Secondary/CTA: Gold Standard (#E9C349)
- Headlines: Noto Serif, tight letter-spacing
- Body: Manrope, generous line-height (1.6)
- NO 1px borders - use tonal color shifts for sections
- Glassmorphism navigation bar with backdrop-blur-md
- Use the full design system: Noto Serif for headlines, Manrope for body, Burgundy (#4E0707) primary, Gold (#D4AF37) secondary, Dark mode with charcoal surface (#131313)

**Page Structure:**
1. Header with VSG Bliss logo and navigation (links to: Home, About, Events, Artists, Contact, Newsletter)
2. Hero section with "The Sound of VSG" headline and featured playlist
3. Featured Artists section with audio player cards (track title, artist name, play button)
4. Recent Releases grid with album artwork and streaming links
5. Curated Playlists section (mood-based collections)
6. Footer with social links and copyright
